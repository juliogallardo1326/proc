What forms of payment do you accept?

Nichebill.com accepts VISA, Mastercard, Debit Cards, e-checks, and Web900.


How often will I be paid?

Payments will be made on Friday the following the pay period.


What is a rolling reserve?

A rolling reserve is a small portion of gross sales held by the bank to ensure that credits and charge backs are handled accurately.


What types of merchants does Nichebill.com provide services for?

Nichebill.com works with all types of merchants, but we specialize in high-risk e-businesses.


Will I need a new application for every website I work with?

No. Nichebill.com has a variety of integration options. Moving your entire book of websites to Nichebill.com is as easy as working with a single site.


What is a Discount Rate?

Each time a charge is processed; a % of the gross approved transaction amount is collected and deducted from your transaction. This is what allows you to process your transactions with the merchant bank.


What is a Transaction Fee?

Each time a transaction is processed by the bank, no matter whether it approves or declines, the bank charges a transaction fee to process the order and fraud scrub it. This fee is passed along to you the merchant on each and every transaction.
