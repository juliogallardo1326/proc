This support system is here to help you manage your customer service better, and post questions of your own.

How to Create a Support Ticket:

  1. Make sure you're in the 'Client Services' section, not the 'Customer Service' section.
  2. Select the 'Create Issue' option towards the top of the screen.
  3. Select your Category (Tech Support, Sales Question...).
  4. Enter a Summary.
  5. Enter the Initial Description of your ticket.
  6. Optionally Upload any files that may be required by the support staff.
  7. Select the 'Submit' Button.

How do you know when you get a response to your Support Ticket?

  • Select 'List Issues' to view your ticket status.
  • We also send out emails each time we respond to your ticket.

How to manage your Customers:

  1. Make sure you're in the 'Customer Service' section, not the 'Client Services' section.
  2. Select the 'List Issues' option towards the top of the screen.
  3. Any customer querys will appear here. Select one to view, edit, update, or respond to them.

How do you know when a customer sends you a Support Ticket?

  • We send out email notifications each time a customer of yours sends us a query.
